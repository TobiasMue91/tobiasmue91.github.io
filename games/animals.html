Pawpurr

A game for your cat. One bug at a time — a buzzing fly, a scuttling spider — moving like the real thing. Lay the tablet flat and let them hunt.

Best flat on the floor in a sturdy case. One target at a time so your cat can lock on. It never ends — close the tab when playtime's over.

Options

For you, not the cat. During play, press & hold the top-right corner to reopen.
Background
Cats see blues & greys best. Dark backgrounds make prey pop.
Creatures in rotation
Turn off any your cat ignores so the rest show more often.
Liveliness
How fast and restless the prey is.
Haptic feedback
Buzz the device when prey is caught.
Sound
The fly's buzz and catch chirps.
Catch difficulty
How long a paw must hold prey to secure the catch.
Session timer
Vets suggest short sessions. Game dims gently when time's up.
