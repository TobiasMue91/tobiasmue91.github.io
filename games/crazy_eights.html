Crazy Eights Rules

Crazy Eights is a classic card game played with a standard deck of 52 cards. The objective is to be the first player to get rid of all your cards.

Setup:
Each player receives 5 cards. The remaining cards form the draw pile, with one card turned face up to start the discard pile.

Gameplay:
Players take turns playing a card that matches either the suit or rank of the top card on the discard pile. If a player cannot play, they must draw a card. If the drawn card can be played, they may play it immediately.

Special Cards:
Eights: When an Eight is played, the player declares a suit that the next player must follow.

Winning:
The first player to get rid of all their cards wins.

Scoring:
When a player wins, they receive points based on the cards left in opponents' hands:
- Eights: 50 points
- Face cards (K, Q, J): 10 points
- Ace: 1 point
- Number cards: face value
