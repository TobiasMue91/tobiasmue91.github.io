The Hollow

They say the maze breathes — that its walls shift when no light touches them, that the warden was once a wanderer like you who let their lantern gutter out and was unmade into hunger.

Keep your lantern fed by the torches bracketed to the walls; between their pools the dark is absolute and the cold gnaws your light away. Three embers burn in every layer. Gather them and the gate to the deeper Hollow opens. Each descent is a different place, and a different cruelty.

The Cisterns — old waterworks, where it all begins.
The Ember Vaults — bright torches, but the warden runs fast and hungry.
The Drowned Deep — near-total darkness; seek the Seer Eyes that reveal the way.
The Whispering Warren — more than one set of eyes. Hush Charms hide you.
The Cold Lattice — freezing; your oil burns away quickly. Hoard the flasks.
