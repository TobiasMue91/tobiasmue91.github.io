              What Is This?

              A LIVING CATALOGUE
              A single person's playground for tiny web things — most of the source written by language models, curated and hand-finished. Everything is open on GitHub.
              NO TRACKING, NO ADS
              No analytics, no accounts, no storage beyond your browser. Links open plain HTML pages. The whole thing is static.
