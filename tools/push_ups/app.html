How to use
  1. Position your device so the camera can see you during push-ups
  2. Press "Calibrate" and do a test push-up for optimal detection
  3. Hit "Start" and begin your workout
  4. For every 10 push-ups, you'll earn 1 token
  5. Use tokens to redeem rewards you've set up

For best results, ensure good lighting and position your device at an angle where your up/down movement is clearly visible.
