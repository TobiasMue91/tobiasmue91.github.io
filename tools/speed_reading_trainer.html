How Speed Reading Works

This tool helps you increase your reading speed while maintaining comprehension through three proven techniques:

RSVP Mode

Words appear one at a time in the center of your vision, eliminating the need for eye movement and greatly increasing speed.

Guided Reading

Text is displayed with a moving focus area that guides your eyes through the text at an optimal pace.

Highlight Mode

Full text is displayed with current word highlighting, helping you maintain a steady pace while seeing context.
